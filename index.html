ℹ️ How it works ▾
🏆
Win challenges — Enter and win challenges in any category. Every win counts toward your rank.
🔄
30-day rolling window — Rankings reset every month. New users have just as much chance to reach #1 as veterans.
🔥
Become the Leader — The #1 ranked player in each category earns the Leader title. Rankings update live as wins come in.
👑
Become Champion — Champion is earned by winning the monthly Championship Challenge — not just by leading the board. It's the highest title on the platform.
⚡
Championship Challenge — On the last day of each month, the top 3 players in a category are invited to a head-to-head Championship Challenge. Win it to claim or defend the Champion title.
🌟
Hall of Fame — Championship titles are recorded permanently. Win a title multiple times and earn an "Nx Champion" badge on your profile forever.
