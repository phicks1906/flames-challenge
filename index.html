Leaderboard

Monthly wins · updated live · ⓘ how it works

🏆
Win challenges — Enter and win challenges in any category. Every win counts toward your rank.
🔄
Monthly rankings — The board resets on the 1st of each month, so every championship starts fresh. New users have just as much chance to reach #1 as veterans.
🔥
Become the Leader — The #1 ranked player in each category earns the Leader title. Rankings update live as wins come in.
👑
Become Champion — Champion is earned by winning the monthly Championship Challenge — not just by leading the board. It's the highest title on the platform.
⚡
Championship Challenge — On the last day of each month, the top 3 players in a category are invited to a head-to-head Championship Challenge. Win it to claim or defend the Champion title.
🌟
Hall of Fame — Championship titles are recorded permanently. Win a title multiple times and earn an "Nx Champion" badge on your profile forever.

📜 Community Guidelines

FlamesChallenge runs on competition and trash talk. Bring the heat — just keep it about the contest, not cruelty. Here's the line.

✅ Bring it
  • Competitive trash talk — "I'll destroy you," "you're getting cooked," "easy work"
  • Confidence, callouts, rivalries, big claims
🚫 Not allowed
  • Slurs or hate speech
  • Real threats of violence or harm to anyone
  • Harassment, bullying, or targeting a person
  • Sexual or explicit content (photos, video, or text)
  • Graphic violence or gore
  • Spam, scams, or impersonation
  • Sharing someone's private info (doxxing)
🔒 Zero tolerance
  • Any sexual content involving minors. Removed immediately, account banned, and reported to the authorities.

See something that crosses the line? Tap the ⋯ on any post and hit Report — we review fast. Breaking these rules can get your content removed and your account suspended.
